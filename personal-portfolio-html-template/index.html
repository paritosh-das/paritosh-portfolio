About Me

Hello! I am Paritosh Das, a passionate and driven Computer Science & Engineering graduate from Siksha ‘O’ Anusandhan University, Bhubaneswar. Prior to that, I completed my Diploma in Engineering from KIIT Polytechnic, Bhubaneswar. Hailing from the beautiful city of Bhubaneswar, Odisha, famously known as the Temple City, I have always been inspired by a blend of tradition and technology.

With a strong foundation in software development, I have a deep passion for exploring emerging technologies and constantly enhancing my skill set. As a software engineer in process, I am always eager to learn, adapt, and innovate. With a strong passion for technology, leadership, and event management, I continue to explore new opportunities and push my boundaries. My goal is to leverage my technical expertise, management skills, and networking abilities to make a meaningful impact in the industry.

Thank you for taking the time to learn about me. I look forward to connecting, collaborating, and growing together!
